Frequently Asked Questions

Are your games free to play?

Yes! All of our casual and hyper-casual titles are completely free to download and play, supported by optional ads and premium in-app purchases.

What platforms are your games available on?

Our games are highly optimized for both major mobile operating systems. You can easily find them on your standard mobile app stores.

Can I collaborate with PixelSpark?

We are always open to exciting partnerships, brand sponsorships, and business inquiries. Reach out to us via the contact form below.

Do you publish games from indie developers?

Currently, we primarily focus on in-house development, but we occasionally co-develop prototypes if the central idea aligns perfectly with our aesthetic.
